BIGGS Sunflower Seed Showdown!

What are the best flavors?

Old Bay Seasoned BIGGS

BIGGS Old Bay Seasoned sunflower seeds are my personal favorite flavor. The first package was mediocre, as was the second. When I eventually decided to give them another try though, something clicked. The flavor of Old Bay Seasoning is so closeley associated with seafood in my mind these remind me of having an amazing seafood boil without actually tasting like seafood. I am forced to order these from Amazon as they are hard to find in person.

Takis Fuego BIGGS

BIGGS Takis Fuego sunflower seeds are a close runner up to the Old Bay Seasoned. These are alot easier to find ase of late due to the massive popularity of Takis tortilla chips. These are very good, the flavor is exactly the same as the chips as is the include heartburn.

Taco Supreme BIGGS

BIGGS Taco Supreme sunflower are the most embarrassing flavor in the lineup, but they are good. These actually have a Taco Bell type of taste to them. Its strange to have the flavor of a Tex-mex taco in a seed but it works.

Similarly to the Old Bay Seasoned seeds, I did not care for these at first. However, they have replaced Dill Pickle BIGGS in the top three line up....for now.
